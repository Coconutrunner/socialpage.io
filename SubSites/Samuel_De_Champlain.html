Samuel De Champlain

Background?

Born Around: Aug 1574, Born in Anius He was a French explorer, navigator, soldier, geographer and colonial administrator. Had a navigators Education,

Why did he explore?

Champlain wanted to explore because he wanted to find new trade routes and he wanted France to grow stronger and richer with fur and land and he also wanted to make maps of the rivers and lakes he saw. He was also curious about the new world and he wanted to bring settlers and make new towns even if it was very hard.

Who Did he Work with?

Champlain worked with King Henry IV and Pierre Dugua de Mons and French settlers to start Quebec but many had hard times in the cold and some died but they kept going. He also worked with the Huron who traded furs and helped him explore and fight wars against the Iroquois which made strong ties but also much fighting.

What Journeny did he take?

Starting out: Exploring the St Lawrence river, around Nova Scotia and new England
1603: Journey to New France/Canada
1608: Made a Quebec Settlement

What was the long and short term impact?

Long Term: Started Quebec City, Made maps of rivers, lakes and the land. Helped france take a hold on canada, Disease, lots of conflict, Traded Goods and knowledge, Wars with English, French, Indigenous Globalization was opened up with the fur trade. Taking advantage of the resources

Short Term: Started a Quebec settlement, Traded goods France connected with North America via trade routes.
